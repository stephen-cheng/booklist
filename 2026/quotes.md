1. Success lay in smiling, showing interest in other people, and making them feel good about themselves. 
2. In our fast-paced, information-overload world of multiple stimuli bombarding us every second, peoples heads are spinning. They must form quick judgments to make sense of the world and get on with what they have to do. So, whenever people meet you, they take an instant mental snapshot. That image of you becomes the data they deal with for a very long time.
3. The way you look and the way you move is more than 80 percent of someone’s first impression of you. Not one word need be spoken.
4. The first few moments of your reactions set the stage upon which the entire relationship will be played out. 
5. To make your conversation partner feel welcome, you must master small talk.
6. 85 percent of ones success in life is directly due to communications skills.
7. People with larger vocabularies get hired quicker, promoted faster, and listened to a whole lot more.
8. Whenever you have something in common with someone, the longer you wait to reveal it, the more moved (and impressed) he or she will be.
9. Whenever you are discussing emotionally charged matters, let the speaker finish completely before you jump in.
10. Excellence is not a single and solitary action. It is the outcome of many years of making small smooth moves.
11. Repeating an action makes a habit. Your habits create your character. And your character is your destiny.
